# Lua Pattern Matching Configuration
# Format: REGEX_PATTERN | script_name.lua | COMMENT
#
# When the BBS sends text matching a pattern, the associated Lua script
# is executed with capture groups available in the match[] array.

# Health monitoring - track HP and auto-heal when low
Your health: (\d+)/(\d+) | health_monitor.lua | Track health and auto-heal

# Planet scanning - track planet numbers and auto-land on target
Planet (\d+) | planet_scan.lua | Track planets and auto-land

# Location tracking - track current location and visit counts
You are in (.+) | location_tracker.lua | Track location visits
